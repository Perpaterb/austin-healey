    Associate Members now available - complete the application form details on the 2017/ 2018 Membership Application form  and post to  the Club's postal address
     
    Membership Criteria
     Membership is open to Members and Associate Members within the requirements of Clause 4 of the Constitution:
     
    a) a Member is a natural person who has proved to the Committee, past or present ownership of a vehicle of the Marque;
    b) an Associate Member is  a financial member who has not been a past owner or is a present owner of a vehicle of the Marque;
    c) A vehicle of the "Marque" - being "All vehicles bearing the Healey name either on its own or in association.";
    and
    d) Prospective full members must submit application for membership in the form provided including proof of ownership.
