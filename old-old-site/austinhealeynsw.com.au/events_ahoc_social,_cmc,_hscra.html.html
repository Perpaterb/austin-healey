    AHOC NSW Inc:  2019 Mid Week Drive 

     

      
      
      
      
      
      

     

     

     

     2019 Mid-Week Drives calendar

    Come along for a fun and friendly run on the 3rd Wednesday of each month (February to November). Additional details will be given each month at the Club meeting the week preceding the run, as well as being posted on the website & included in the newsletter.

     

    Mid-Week Drive – Wednesday 20th February 2019
    The destination for the first mid-week drive for 2019
    will be the northern Sydney beachside suburb of
    Palm Beach.
    The lunch venue on the day will be The Boathouse,
    located in Governor Phillip Park at Palm Beach.
    We are meeting either at 10am at the café within Flower
    Power Nursery, 279 Mona Vale Road, Terrey Hills
    or at 12 noon at Palm Beach for lunch.
    In case you are unaware, the Flower Power Nursery
    used to be Bond’s Nursery, the past proprietor of
    which was our own Ross Bond, now the patron of our
    club.
    After leaving Flower Power, it’s a 30 km journey
    along Mona Vale Road until we turn left onto McCarrs
    Creek Road. Then it’s along Pittwater Road and Barrenjoey
    Road (via Church Point) until we reach Palm
    Beach. Continue past the ferry terminal (on your left)
    until you reach Governor Phillip Park. Drive along the
    park until you come to The Boathouse which will be
    on your left.
    Lunch will be at 12 noon and we are unable to make
    a reservation, on arrival please organise your own
    seating.
    If you would like to join with your fellow club members
    please let Margaret and Geoff Smith by phone on
    0438 414 700 or m.gpsmith@bigpond.com by Friday
    15th February 2019.

    Please note:

    • To make sure that you don’t miss out on either lunch or tours, please contact the organisers in the week leading up to the run.
    • Due to extenuating circumstances, sometimes destinations may need to be altered. Any changes will be notified at the meeting, on the website and in the newsletter.
    • Cancellations may occasionally occur due to inclement weather.
    • The organisers are always keen to learn of new drives and venues. Please don’t hesitate to share your great ideas!
