    • About the Club

     
    The Austin-Healey Owners Club (NSW) Inc. has been an organisation since 1969 dedicated to the pursuit of happiness and fun through the love and enjoyment of owning a Healey or Austin-Healey sports car.

    Membership is open to current and past owners of Healey (Donald Healey Motor Company produced vehicles) and Austin-Healey as well as other vehicles owned by Club members. Proof of ownership (copy of registration or receipt) should accompany all applications for membership.

    The basic aims of the Club are to maintain and preserve vehicles of the marque in the best possible condition and to encourage members to participate in competition, social and informative events.

    Organised activities are many and varied, ranging from social functions, technical days, competitive events (as a member of the Combined Sports Car Association), drives, Concours d'Elegance to National Rallies organised in turn by each Australian Club.

    In addition, membership benefits include access to the Club's spare parts, technical assistance, a library of historical and technical literature, service and restoration advice, regalia, a monthly FLAT-CHAT newsletter and the opportunity to meet and develop lasting friendships with other enthusiasts who share a love of Healey and Austin-Healey cars.

    For more information and to see what the Club has to offer please come along to our monthly meetings, where visitors and prospective new members always receive a very warm welcome, or contact our Membership Secretary.
