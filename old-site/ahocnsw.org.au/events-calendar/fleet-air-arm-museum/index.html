Fleet Air Arm Museum Drive Day

26 September 2025 @ 09:30

AHOC NSW Drive Day
Fleet Air Arm Museum

Hosted by AHOC NSW Illawarra and Southern Highlands Chapter

Friday 26 September 2025
Time: 9:30am start
RSVP Now

Event Details

Meet: 9:30am at the iconic Berry Donut Van, 73 Queen Street, Berry – for coffee and donuts.

Destination: Fleet Air Arm Museum, HMAS Albatross, 489A Albatross Road, Nowra Hill (approx. 30-minute drive from Berry).

Lunch: Worrigee Sports Club Complex (also known as The Growers or Worrigee Golf Club), Greenwell Point Road. Lunch and social catch-up from about 12:30–1:00pm.

About this event
This will be a great opportunity to enjoy our Healeys on the open road, explore Australia’s naval aviation history, and share good company over lunch.

We hope to see many of you there – Healeys preferred, but all cars are welcome!
